"Mechanism for crisis response of the civil sector in a municipality Varna"

The project aims to empower the civil sector in the municipality of Varna by creating a community and communication that support the organization of activities in the sector. Through this project, citizens are given the opportunity to be informed about different ways in which they can support the initiatives that are being developed in the city. The main goal of the project is to strengthen the interaction between organizations and citizens in the municipality of Varna and to stimulate cooperation between them. By creating partnerships and developing joint projects, civil sector organizations will have the opportunity to improve the effectiveness and reach of their initiatives for the benefit of the community. This project represents an important step in strengthening civic participation and activity in the municipality of Varna. By creating a crisis response mechanism, the civil sector will be better equipped to deal with challenges and crises that may affect the city and its residents. With your support and commitment, we can achieve positive change and create a better future for the community in Varna Municipality
